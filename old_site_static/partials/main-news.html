Welcome to EveKit!

EveKit is a web-based toolkit for building applications based on the EVE Online third party APIs. EveKit provides the following services (use the toolbar above to switch between services):
Account Synchronization Services Keep an up to date copy of your character and corporation data on the web, with convenient APIs for access from your own applications.
EVE Static Data Export Online Access the latest static data export online using our REST interfaces, or easily generate a client library in your favorite language.
EVE Market Data Quickly view recent or historic order books or market data, or download data for your own use.
These services combine to provide a rich set of capabilities which can be used in a variety of different third party applications.

Key features:

EveKit services are web-ready. Every API is annotated with Swagger markup making it easy to work directly with REST services (returning JSON), or generate a client in the language of your choice (using free tools like the Swagger Editor).
EveKit account synchronization can download for both characters and corporations, at least as frequently as the EVE API cache timers allow.
Account synchronization keeps a complete history of everything. Want to know what your assets looked like a month ago? No problem. The account model APIs allow you to access your data at a date you specify (defaulting to the latest live data).
Account data we store on your behalf can be tagged with persistent meta data. This makes it easy to store custom data you might need for certain applications right next to your regular account data.
EveKit provides interfaces to key reference data like the EVE Static Data Export and EVE Market Data. We always provide access to the last two SDE dumps. We scrape market data continuously (at least as frequently as the cache timers allow), and generate daily dump files for download.
Any account data we store on your behalf is always available to you in bulk form (as CSV files). We recognize that not everyone will want to use our APIs to access their data, so we generate daily snapshots which you can access from our account synchronization services. We don't trap your data behind our APIs, you can get a complete snapshot any time you like.
Use the toolbar to select one of our services to get started. If you need help, or want to explore more of our documentation, check out our Guides, Tutorials and Videos!
