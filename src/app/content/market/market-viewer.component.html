Market Data View

EveKit captures regular snapshots of EVE Online market data, including both order books and market history. Two types of access are provided:

  • Live REST API: EveKit provides a REST API which also includes an OpenAPI specification. You can generate a client in your favorite language using tools like Swagger CodeGen or the Swagger Editor and the specification URL shown below
  • Archive: At the close of each day (midnight UTC), EveKit packages all order books and market history into a convenient format for bulk retrieval. These files are publicly available on the cloud.

The "live" service is best for interactive tools where specific market information is needed for explicit types or regions. This service is not efficient for bulk or large volume retrieval. The "archive" service is intended to support bulk retrieval.
